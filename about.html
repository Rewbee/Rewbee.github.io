About

Ruby Watt is a freelance illustrator currently based in Ecuador. 

I am a 19-year-old freelance Illustrator/Web Designer and graduate of the Multimedia & Interactive Technology program at Skagit Valley College. I create trendy/ stylish artwork with my own twist. I am actively involved in volunteer service in my community and abroad to help others and to make a difference in the world.

    My process

    Process 1

    I find a image of a landscape, person, and or objects that I find interesting. I also use my own images as well. 

    Process 2

    The I look at what interestes me and why? Is it the shapes, the colors, the persons look?     

    Process 3

    Then I start with what interestes me and make it my own and add my own twist to it. Like this picture I draw on the left. I found it on pinterest, the images had color in the eyes, a stronger nose, and brighter colors. Now, its in more of my style.
