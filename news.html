Introducing Me

A drawing charater drawing that repersents me, ‎November ‎13, ‎2020,

Hi, my name is Ruby Watt. I am a 19-year-old inspiring artist/freelancer; currently located South America. Looking to share my journey through writing and art. I create Digital Art by using the program called Clip Studio Paint or what I like to call it CSP. I first started out by putting different images together and tracing them to make a whole image. Now, I am getting back into free hand drawing and trying to find my style. What I am hoping to do with this blog is to share how I started with my digital art journey with those who are starting out just like me.

Artwork of Ecuadorian Children

Children of Ecuador, September 2, 2017

These projects I created was during this quarantine. My mother asked me to create these images for children in our area for some encouragement. So I drew them with their favorite animal. We have a Tiger, Monkey, and a Condor. Condor’s are from this area and you can see them flying around in the secluded areas. The Condor is the largest raptor in the world. Their wingspan of over 3 meters (10 feet). So the Condor in the the picture could definitely fly way with him. I realized that we need to reach back and find our childhood innocents again. As a child, what would you say your favorite animal is and why?

Koi Fish

Koi fish swimming in a colorful background, October ‎13, ‎2020

I drew the fish to represent new beginnings, because I wanted to start new on my art style. To create something of mine and only mine.
